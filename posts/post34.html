When it comes to that upset stomach of yours, it is vital that one understands how cranial nerve 10, Histamine 2, the parietal cell and proton pump work not only to produce the acid, sterilize your food, absorb B12 and even absorb that infamous vitamin D.

So here is just part of your homework for people who just love understanding the basics of how your body works instead of just attending lectures hearing people speak of their lofty credentials and getting paid quite well. Look up a molecule called "intrinsic factor" and why you need that acid in the right place (the stomach) to absorb vitamin B12. Vitamin B12 is used in the dorsal columns of your BRAIN and also in RED BLOOD CELLS to maintain balance, (brain), but help red blood cells carry OXYGEN to THE BRAIN, (energy).

So when the brain is slowly robbed of its substance,(dorsal coulumn), and OXYGEN, RBC dysfunction known as megaloblastic anemia, say over 5 years, a decrease of oxygen and substance in the brain can lead to forms of DEMENTIA, (loss of substance and energy).
