Hair, Skin and NAILS

"It is time to have a talk about IRON DEFICIENCY ANEMIA and also time for you to eat more spinach."

Posted by Dr. Colin Ross on June 15, 2022

Ladies and gentlemen, if your manicurist ever sees a fingernail like this one in the picture below, it is time to have a talk about IRON DEFICIENCY ANEMIA and also time for you to eat more spinach with other green leafy vegetables and/or see your primary care clinician.

Photo by Dr. Colin Ross

Once upon a time, part of a patient's Physical Exam included a visual examination of hair, skin and NAILS instead of just advising the patient to take the multivitamin called Hair Skin and Nails.

Remember the word 'Doctor' means 'Teacher', so I taught my manicurist to teach her clients how to recognize a potential metabolic disorder using her expertise of seeing nails on a daily basis.
